Barcode Masters - Monster Battle Game

Game Overview

In Barcode Masters, you scan "The Codes" â€” special barcodes that hold the DNA essence of different creatures. When you scan a Code, you unlock a unique one of a kind creature that appears with it's stats, level, and random starter name. Collect and train your creatures, then battle them to rise to the top!

How to Play

  • Scan The Code: Use your barcode scanner to unlock creatures. Each scan will give you a creature with stats, level, and experience.
  • View and Rename: Go to the Collection Manager to view your creatures, rename them, and track their progress.
  • Battle: Enter PVP battles and tournaments to test your creatures' strength against others.
  • Energy Management: Keep an eye on your energy to control when you can scan for new creatures or buy power-ups.

Energy System

Energy is used to scan new creatures. More powerful creatures require more energy to unlock. You can buy energy power-ups to help you in your journey, and be strategic with your energy usage to collect and grow the best monsters!

Creature Evolution

As your creatures battle and gain experience, they level up and evolve. They become stronger and unlock new abilities, giving you even more power in battles!
